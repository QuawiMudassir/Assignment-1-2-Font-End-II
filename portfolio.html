I collaborated closely with cross-functional teams, bringing my creative vision to life by integrating cutting-edge design principles and user-centered methodologies. My expertise went beyond aesthetics; I understood the importance of creating layouts that were not only visually stunning but also highly intuitive, allowing users to effortlessly explore event venues and find the information they needed.
